Voice-first · Offline-first · Rural healthcare

ASHA AI replaces writing with voice, paperwork with automation, and delay with coordinated local action.

01

NGO & Government Analytics Dashboard

District and village-level insights: high-risk clusters, SOS frequency, follow-up gaps, and campaign effectiveness. Aggregated, never personal.

  • Smarter resource allocation
  • Spot vulnerable areas early
  • Measure campaign impact
02

Voice-First Healthcare Assistant

Women simply speak about their health in their local language. No typing, no forms. ASHA AI listens, understands, and responds with simple guidance.

  • Works for low-literacy users
  • Removes language barriers
  • Reduces hesitation and stigma
03

Paperless Voice Logging for ASHA Workers

"Visited Sunita today. She feels weak and dizzy." One sentence becomes a health record, an updated history, and a visit summary.

  • No paperwork, no typing
  • Faster reporting
  • More time for patient care
No writing. Just voice.
04

Offline-First Healthcare Infrastructure

Voice recordings, health records, follow-ups, and SOS alerts are stored locally. When internet returns, everything syncs automatically.

  • Keeps working with zero connectivity
  • Built for rural network conditions
  • Automatic background sync
05

Red Zone Emergency SOS System

For severe bleeding, labor distress, or intense pain: one press triggers a coordinated local response.

  • Nearby ASHA workers alerted first
  • Escalates to NGOs if no response
  • Works even without internet
06

CareScore Risk Detection Engine

A 0–100 risk score built from symptom history, missed checkups, follow-up behavior, and pregnancy stage, so high-risk women are found early.

  • Early warning, not diagnosis
  • Prioritized visit lists for ASHAs
  • Tracks patterns over time
07

ASHA-to-ASHA & NGO Coordination Network

If one ASHA worker is unavailable, another nearby can respond, and NGOs are looped in when needed.

  • No woman left waiting
  • Shared local response
  • NGO backup built in
Individual workers become a connected care network.
